Privacy Policy for Polar Shift

Last updated: July 26, 2025

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use Polar Shift (“the App”).

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly.

However, the App may use third-party services that collect limited anonymous data, such as:

  • Apple (App Store, In-App Purchase APIs)
  • Unity or Godot Analytics (if enabled)
  • Crash reporting tools (e.g., Apple crash logs)

2. In-App Purchases

When you make a purchase, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment or billing information.

3. Ads

The free version of the App may show ads. Ads are delivered by third-party providers, which may collect anonymous usage data in compliance with their privacy policies.

Once you purchase the full version, all ads are permanently removed.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

5. Your Rights

You may delete the app at any time to stop data collection. You may also contact Apple to manage purchase data or analytics tracking.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
